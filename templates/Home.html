Welcome to Neway Challenge Academy Absence Request Portal!

Easily request permission for a day off with just a few simple steps.

Request Absence

Follow the steps below to request permission for a day off:

  1. Please go to the Ask permission page.
  2. Fill out the absence request form with your information and reason for absence.
  3. Attach any necessary documentation (e.g., doctor's note, family emergency details).
  4. Submit the form for review by the school administration.

Rules and Regualtions

  1. Any kind of illegal document will immedialely result in suspension.
  2. Your request will be reviewed by the admin and you will recieve an email.
  3. The email that is sent contains a document that needs to be printed.
  4. You can use the document to show your teachers and staff members which they will immediately approve your request.
  5. The permission letter is only legible for 7 days after the day it is sent.
  6. Only 2 requested are allowed per month.
